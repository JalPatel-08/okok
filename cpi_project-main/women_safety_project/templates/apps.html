Safety Apps & Resources

Find a range of apps and resources to help you stay safe, whether you're looking for emergency assistance, legal help, or self-defense tips.

Government Websites

  • National Commission for Women - A government body dedicated to protecting and promoting women's rights in India.

  • SHE Teams - An initiative by the Telangana Police focusing on women's safety, particularly in cases of harassment and stalking.

  • Ministry of Women and Child Development - The Indian government ministry dedicated to promoting the welfare of women and children, including various safety initiatives.

  • Cyber Crime Reporting Portal - A portal for reporting online harassment, cybercrime, and fraud.

  • 112 India Emergency Number - Official website for India's emergency response system, providing key information on contacting emergency services.

  • Sakhi One Stop Centres - Government initiative providing integrated support to women affected by violence, including medical, legal, and counseling services.
