About Women's Safety

Statistics



Statistics


According to a recent survey:


  • 60% of women have experienced harassment in public spaces.
  • 75% of women feel unsafe walking alone at night.
  • 40% of women reported workplace harassment.


Key Issues


  • Harassment in public spaces
  • Workplace safety
  • Online harassment
  • Gender-based violence
  • Access to legal support


How to Stay Safe


Preventive measures for staying safe in different environments:


  • Always be aware of your surroundings.
  • Trust your instincts; if something feels off, leave the area.
  • Use well-lit and populated routes.
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible.
  • Share your location with someone you trust when going out.
